Top 3 Highlights in Botswana

Okavango Delta

Flooding to over 15,000 square kilometres, the Okavango Delta is the world's largest inland Delta. The flood waters span out onto land and not water, making it an unquestionably unique destination.

Chobe National Park

Visit home to the largest population of elephants on Earth - over 120 000. Chobe Supports among the greatest concentrations of game in all of Africa.

Cresta Mowana Safari Resort
